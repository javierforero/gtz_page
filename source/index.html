GTZ OVERVIEW

Getting to Zero is a state-wide initiative to end the HIV epidemic in Illinois by 2027. Through partnerships with community-based organizations, the Illinois department of Public Health and other non-profits throughout the state, we can see a dramatic shift to HIV in Illinois.

GOAL & VISION

We want to make sure that the HIV epidemic is no longer able to sustain itself by achieving both HIV prevention and access to care goals.

We want to see:

1. Zero new HIV infections 2. Zero people living with HIV

Through increasing access and uptake of PrEP (pre-exposure prophylaxis), retaining more people living with HIV in care and the continued funding of ongoing supportive services, we can get to zero.
